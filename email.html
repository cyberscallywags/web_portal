Welcome to the Pack

Hey there, Scallywag! Im the Chief Gopherpart guide, part glue, and full-time cheerleader. Youve joined a community thats open, supportive, and just the right amount of mischievous. We learn together, ship together, and have fun doing it.

What we stand for

  • Open & welcoming: Beginners to experts, tech and non-tech, all welcome.
  • Supportive mentorship: Skill-building without egopairing, code review, and guided practice.
  • Have fun, ship value: We enjoy the process while delivering outcomes that matter.
  • Lead from behind: Wolf-pack ethoscollaboration, care, and shared wins.
  • Do good with tech: Community-minded projects (gov/charity, open data, digital inclusion).

Where you fit

Technical

  • Python, FastAPI, Streamlit, Neo4j
  • GraphRAG & knowledge graphs
  • Data pipelines: Kafka, Spark, MinIO
  • DevOps: Docker, CI/CD, IaC

Non-Technical

  • Community ops & moderation
  • Content, design, branding
  • Project & product support
  • Events, outreach & partnerships

What we do together

  • Real projects: From the DSF Companion to data governance tools (RRD, IAR, audit logging).
  • Learning sprints: Hands-on sessionsentity resolution, GraphRAG, streaming vs. batch.
  • Show-and-ship: Demos, write-ups, and dashboards that the community can actually use.
  • Mentor circles: Small groups for portfolio building, talks, and career growth.

Our promise to you

We maintain an explicit Code of Conduct and a mentorship-first culture. You should always feel safe to ask questions, propose ideas, and take the lead. We celebrate kindness, curiosity, and craft.
